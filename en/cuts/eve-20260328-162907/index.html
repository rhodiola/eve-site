cut2 Farewell to the Existing World

The enormous ship
struck into the silence of this planet
and stopped, as if it had lost its breath.

Around the ruptured hull
lie countless androids—
the ones who were supposed to have
traveled the same path.

And at the center, only I
am not yet fully done with ending.
The sand against my cheek is cold—
not pain.

I look up at the sky. A door is there.
White,
uncanny,
filled only with a strange, alien light—
a doorway to another dimension.

It doesn’t look like salvation.
It’s only the outside of this world,
quietly standing open.

My companions are silent,
the ship will never move again.
I have no reason to remain here.
And I suppose—
I will end up choosing it.
